> 高原的氧气稀薄，让章决呼吸困难。陈泊桥三个字一共二十五笔，章决每落一笔，指间到手腕都酸楚一遍。  
> *The thin air of the highlands left Zhang Jue breathless. Chen Boqiao’s name requires twenty-five strokes.With each stroke he wrote, a dull ache spread from his fingers to his wrist.*

> 在罗什的草坪边暗自寻觅陈泊桥的章决，拿着就诊卡站在医院前厅的章决，在长明灯池边写下陈泊桥名字的章决也被好好储存起来了。放进一个很好、很美的，能融化痛楚的房间。  
> *The Zhang Jue who once looked for Chen Boqiao in silence by Roche’s lawn, who stood alone in the hospital foyer clutching his registration card, who wrote Chen Boqiao’s name by the pool of everlasting lamps — he has been stored away, tenderly, in a room that is kind and beautiful, a room warm enough to dissolve all sorrow.*
